A Semester in Review

How Would I Design A Version Of This Course

I believe that the format for this course provided ample materials to help understand the topics that needed to be learned. The textbook provided detailed information and examples on the topics they were trying to present. It provided a good basis for the projects that were assigned to us and encouraged following along with the code examples in our local editor to follow along. I think the daily labs are good exercises to try out the information learned from the assigned readings, but sometimes class time wasn't enough time to finish said labs. I think providing solution sets for the labs may be helpful, or provide more full exampls showcasing what the lab was trying to get across. The current design of the course is very effective in teaching students the fundamental information needed for Web Development in my opinion.

What Helped Me Learn The Material And What Would Help Students Learn?

Following along with each chapter is crucial as most chapters build off eachother. As we get on to later chapters, it builds off the knowledge of earlier chapters which may be challenging if an earlier concept was not fully grasped. For example, a working web app and service can only seem so great when the developer doesn't have a great understanding of CSS and how to present the service to the user. The combination of the readings and labs really helped cement topics into my head for future use, even when I couldn't fully complete the labs. Also, looking up videos on youtube on certain topics may be beneficial for a different explanation on topics that you may not be getting.
