The Importance of Web Development for Businesses

The internet has become a staple to everyday life, allowing us to find the information we need within a few clicks. We've visited all kinds of websites and have come across some that are not to user friendly or inviting. Personally, when I come across a new website that is not put together very well, I start to question the reputation and legitimacy of the site. An article by Alfred Winston, titled "Why Website Design & Development is Important and How it Helps in Making your Business Profitable?", he discusses several results that companies should strive for when creating a website. Companies strive to increase their sales by continuously attracting customers to their website which means the website needs to be user friendly.

Creating an effective website can help business tactics in comparison to only having a physical store, as everything now a adays is online. Winston lists off some major benefits of website developement such as making navigation easy, optimizing for search engines, and streamlining the brand. The company can reach a far greater audience using effective web development strategies and advertising. Simple visual features, such as using certain colors, fonts, images can sway the opinion of a first time user.
