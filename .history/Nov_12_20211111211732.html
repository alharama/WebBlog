Creating an App using the Spotify algorithm

As we come to the end of our time in CS347 Web development, I'm starting to understand the process better of creating Web services and applications. With this newfound knowledge I've already begun thinking of projects that I could explore in my own time that feed to my interests and hobbies. When quarantine first began, I attempted to create a sports statistics web application, but became quickly discouraged as I head no experience in web development. When looking through examples online of sports web services, I stumbled upon a real time sports application tutorial by Jamie Munro. He uses Node.js, Socket.io, and MySportsFeed.com to display live game scores across the NHL.
