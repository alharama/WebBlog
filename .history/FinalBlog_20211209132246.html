A Semester in Review

How Would I Design A Version Of This Course

I believe that the format for this course provided ample materials to help understand the topics that needed to be learned. The textbook provided detailed information and examples on the topics they were trying to present. It provided a good basis for the projects that were assigned to us and encouraged following along with the code examples in our local editor to follow along. I think the daily labs are good exercises to try out the information learned from the assigned readings, but sometimes class time wasn't enough time to finish said labs. I think providing solution sets for the labs may be helpful, or provide more full exampls showcasing what the lab was trying to get across.
